Practice sandbox

Dynamics-style CRM simulator

A simplified, fictional service-desk interface to explore safely. Pick a queue, open a case, assign it to yourself, add a CLEAR note, then escalate or resolve. All organisations, people and data are invented — nothing here is real, and it resets when you reload.

Note: This resembles a professional Customer Service interface for learning only. Real employers customise forms, fields, queues, routing and permissions — always follow your employer's documented procedures.
